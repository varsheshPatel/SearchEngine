        Wikimedia Foundation Governance Wiki

        Welcome to the Wikimedia Foundation Governance Wiki. This wiki is where the Wikimedia Foundation makes publicly available governance materials such as:

        • Board of Trustees documentation
          • Minutes
          • Resolutions
        • Legal documentation
        • Organization policies
        • Security documentation

        Individuals with a registered Wikimedia account are able to edit user and talk pages as well as contribute to translations.
