Follow creative best practices for video ads.

  • Grab attention in the first 3 seconds of your video.
  • Keep videos to 15 seconds or less.
  • Delight with sound on, but add captions for viewers who have their sound off.
  • Ensure your video can be cropped for different formats.

FAQs

What should I know as a first time advertiser?

When setting up your first ad, consider both budget allocation and duration. Understanding both will help you control costs and maximize results.

Budget allocation: You can set budgets for individual ads or entire campaigns. Find the method that works best for your business.

Budget duration: You can set a timeframe for your ad or campaign. We recommend running your campaign for at least 7 days to find the audience that's right for your ads. Finally, you can set a daily or lifetime limit on your spend so you only use what's in your budget.

Learn about budget fundamentals

Easily manage all your accounts at once with Meta Business Suite. Respond to comments, messages, and create posts and ads for Facebook and Instagram, all in one place.
